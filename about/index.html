what is this?
it’s like a little charity but the money just goes straight to people who seem like they could use it. it’s like GiveDirectly, but without any oversight or scientifically backed results. pls donate!!

why?
we believe that creating significant change for individuals is better than creating infinitesimal change for everyone. or at least, it’s more fun, and more achievable for the average person.

how? the logistics aren’t actually that bad. setting the website up took a moment, and there’s some upkeep (checking form responses, sending out payments), but it’s all quite doable. email us if you have specific questions: team@onethingless.net
